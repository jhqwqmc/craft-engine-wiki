---
title: 🕯️ 发光家具
id: glowing_furniture
---

你可以像这样为所有变体统一配置光源位置和亮度等级

```yaml
furniture:
  default:candelabrum:
    behaviors:
      type: glowing_furniture
      lights:
        - position: 0,0,0
          level: 15
```

或者为每个变体单独配置光源位置

```yaml
furniture:
  default:candelabrum:
    behaviors:
      type: glowing_furniture
      variants:
        ground_lit:
          - position: 0,0,0
            level: 15
        wall_lit:
          - position: 0,0,0.3
            level: 15
        ceiling_lit:
          - position: 0,-1,0
            level: 15
```
