---
title: 🔘 按钮方块
id: button_block
---

| 属性名称    | 属性类型    | 是否必需 |
|---------|---------|------|
| powered | boolean | 是    |

```yaml
blocks:
  default:palm_button:
    behavior:
      type: button_block
      ticks-to-stay-pressed: 30
      can-be-activated-by-arrows: true
      sounds:
        on: minecraft:block.wooden_button.click_on
        off: minecraft:block.wooden_button.click_off
```
