---
title: ➡️ 定向附着方块
id: directional_attached_block
---

| 属性名称   | 属性类型                 | 是否必需 |
|--------|----------------------|------|
| facing | horizontal_direction | 是    |

```yaml
blocks:
  default:amethyst_wall_torch:
    behavior:
      type: directional_attached_block
      blacklist: true
      attached-blocks:
        - custom:xxx
        - minecraft:stone
      attached-block-tags:
        - minecraft:dirt
        - minecraft:farmland
```
